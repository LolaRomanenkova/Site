About us

Boasting a private beach area and restaurant-bar alternatives, Biblos Resort Alacati is situated in Alacati. Featuring a 24-hour front desk service. Hotel also provides to guests 8 swimming pool. All units has air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kettle, free toiletries and a desk. Free WiFi is available to all guests, while some rooms come with a terrace. Guest rooms have a wardrobe. A continental, buffet or à la carte breakfast can be enjoyed at the property. The hotel offers a children's playground. Erythrai Antique City is 7 km from Biblos Resort Alacati. The nearest airport is Izmir Adnan Menderes Airport, 67 km from the property. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.0 for a two-person trip. We speak your language!
